Frequently Asked Questions

How do I open a trading account? expand_more
To open an account, click the 'Sign Up' button on the homepage, fill in your personal details, and complete the KYC verification process. Most accounts are approved within 24 hours.
What are the trading fees? expand_more
Our fees vary based on trading volume. Standard taker fees start at 0.1%, while maker fees start at 0.08%. High-volume traders may qualify for reduced rates.
How long do withdrawals take? expand_more
Crypto withdrawals are typically processed within 15-30 minutes. Bank transfers may take 1-3 business days depending on your region and bank.
Can I trade on my mobile phone? expand_more
Yes, we have native apps for both iOS and Android. You can also access our web platform through any mobile browser.
Is my data secure? expand_more
We use industry-standard AES-256 encryption for all data storage and SSL/TLS for data transmission. 95% of digital assets are stored in cold wallets.
How do I enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)? expand_more
Navigate to Account Settings > Security. We support Google Authenticator, Authy, and hardware keys like Yubikey.
What is the minimum trade size? expand_more
The minimum trade size for most pairs is equivalent to $10 USD.
Do you offer institutional accounts? expand_more
Yes, we have a dedicated desk for institutional traders and high-net-worth individuals. Contact our sales team for more details.
What countries are supported? expand_more
We operate in over 150 countries. However, due to regulatory restrictions, we do not currently offer services in certain jurisdictions.
Can I reset my password? expand_more
Yes, click "Forgot Password" on the login page. For security, withdrawals will be disabled for 24 hours after a password reset.
