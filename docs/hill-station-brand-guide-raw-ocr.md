Brand
Strategy
Vision
To be the premier destination in Plateau State, seamlessly blending the rustic charm of its rich heritage
with the sophistication of modern amenities to provide a unique and memorable experience for both
business and leisure travelers.
Mission
To offer exceptional hospitality that harmoniously integrates the warmth of Plateau's culture with
unparalleled service excellence, thereby creating lasting memories and fostering strong community
connections.
Positioning
The Hill Station Hotel is positioned as a gateway to the adventure and culture of Plateau State, providing
a sanctuary of comfort where history meets contemporary elegance. Designed with the theme of "Rustic
Elegance," the hotel offers a unique blend of traditional charm and modern amenities, targeting
adventure seekers and business travelers alike. It serves as the perfect base for exploring the beauty
and diversity of the region while ensuring every guest feels at home.

the image that closely match a color in our

the image, it's best to choose a more
neutral color that doesn't feel too jarring
with the image. These tend to be blues and
greens, or Sand. Make sure there's enough
contrast between the image and

In order to ensure The Hill Station
photography is cohesive and high quality,
our imagery utilizes a set of three guiding
principles:
1. Optimistic
2. Authentic
3. Dynamic
Use these principles as a checklist when
capturing photos or selecting imagery to
be used for the Hill Station brand.
All photographs should have an editorial
feel. They should capture a moment, tell a
story, and feel natural. They should not
appear overly stock or disingenuous.
